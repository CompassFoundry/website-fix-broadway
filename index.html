Broadway Street NE is UNSAFE. We want to fix it. Here are the steps you can take to help us!

Join a task force comprised of neighborhood associations, NE residents, and NE commuters by sharing feedback about your experiences with Broadway Street NE. Once we collect enough feedback, we'll contact our local city and county representatives who are responsible for making roads, pedestrian walkways, and bike lanes safe for everyone.
